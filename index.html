What are these Erectus ?

FuseErectus is a collection of 2222 Erectus unique digital collectibles on the Fuse blockchain. Which is 2nd serie of Evolution after Celo Erectus collection. Each Erectus is unique and programmatically generated from over 75 traits, including Hat, clothing, and more. All Erectus are dope, but some are rarer than others.

The Erectus are stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Fuse blockchain. Join our Evolution.
